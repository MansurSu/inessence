Meet Ines Govaerts

Passionate about bodywork, healing, and artistic expression, Ines has dedicated her life to the balance between physical well-being and creative energy.

From an early age, Ines was drawn to the power of touch and movement, leading her to explore the ancient practice of Thai massage. Over the years, she has blended this knowledge with herbal healing, poetry, and bodywork, creating a holistic approach that nurtures both body and soul.

Whether through the rhythm of poetry, the grounding energy of plant-based medicine, or the deep connection of body therapy, Ines seeks to create a space where healing and creativity come together.
